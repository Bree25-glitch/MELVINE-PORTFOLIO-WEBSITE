About Me

Who I Am

>

Hello, my name is Melvine Okeyo, and I am a passionate and dedicated web developer. I enjoy creating clean, modern, and user-friendly websites that provide a great experience for users.
I have been learning and working with HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript, and I am continuously improving my skills by building real projects such as travel and cooking websites.
Through these projects, I have developed a strong understanding of layout design, responsiveness, and web structure. I am highly motivated to grow in the tech industry, and I believe in learning by doing.
Every project I create helps me become better, more creative, and more confident in my abilities. I am excited about future opportunities where I can contribute, learn, and build impactful digital solutions.

My Journey

My journey into web development started with a curiosity about how websites are made. I began by learning HTML and CSS, which allowed me to create simple static pages.
As I gained confidence, I started exploring JavaScript to add interactivity to my projects.
I have built several projects, including a travel agency website that features responsive design and a cooking recipe site that allows users to search for recipes based on ingredients.
These projects have been instrumental in helping me understand the practical applications of web development concepts.
I am committed to continuous learning and growth in this field, and I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead as I continue to develop my skills and contribute to meaningful projects.

My Values

I believe in the power of creativity and innovation in web development. I am passionate about creating websites that are not only visually appealing but also functional and user-friendly.
I value continuous learning and improvement, and I am always looking for ways to enhance my skills and stay updated with the latest trends in web development.
I am committed to building projects that have a positive impact on users and contribute to the digital landscape in meaningful ways.
